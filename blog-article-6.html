Mobile App Beta: Manage Your Balance on the Go

June 15, 2026 · 5 min read

We're excited to announce the beta launch of the GlbTOKEN mobile web app — a fully responsive, browser-based experience that brings the full power of your GlbTOKEN account to your phone, tablet, or any mobile device. No app store download required, no installation, no updates to manage. Simply open your browser, log in, and you're ready to go.

Over the past several months, we've heard a clear message from our users: managing AI token balances, monitoring usage, and buying more tokens shouldn't require sitting at a desktop. Whether you're a developer checking API usage between meetings, a freelancer topping up from a coffee shop, or a team lead approving budget increases on the go — you need access to your GlbTOKEN account wherever you are. Our mobile web app delivers exactly that.

Why a Mobile Web App?

You might be asking: why a mobile web app instead of a native iOS/Android app? The answer comes down to three core principles we believe in at GlbTOKEN: speed, accessibility, and zero friction.

Zero installation. Native apps require downloading, installing, waiting for updates, and managing storage. A progressive web app (PWA) loads instantly from your browser. Open Safari, Chrome, or any modern mobile browser, navigate to glbtoken.com, log in — and you have the full experience. For Android users, you can even "Add to Home Screen" for a native-style icon and full-screen experience.

Instant updates. When we ship a new feature or fix a bug, it's live immediately. No waiting for app store review cycles that can take days. No prompting users to download version 2.4.3. The mobile web app is always up to date because it's just a web page.

Cross-platform by design. Whether you use an iPhone, a Samsung Galaxy, a Pixel, a tablet, or even a foldable — it works. One codebase, every device. We didn't want to build an iOS app, then an Android app, then a tablet version, and still leave out Windows Phone or KaiOS users. The mobile web app works everywhere.

No data sync headaches. Native apps often need to sync data between devices. With a web app, you just log in. Your dashboard, your balance, your API keys — they're all there, instantly, because the data lives on our servers, not on your device. Switch between your phone and laptop seamlessly.

What You Can Do in the Mobile App

The mobile web app includes nearly every feature from the desktop dashboard, carefully reorganized for smaller screens. Here's what's available in the beta:

Full Dashboard. View your current token balance, recent transactions, and usage history at a glance. The dashboard is optimized for mobile with larger tap targets, swipeable cards, and a streamlined layout that puts the most important information front and center. Your token balance is prominently displayed at the top, and recent API usage is visualized in a compact chart that works beautifully on small screens.

Buy Tokens. The top-up flow has been completely redesigned for mobile. Select an amount, choose your payment method (credit card, USDT, BTC, or Paystack for African users), and complete the purchase — all within the browser. We've integrated Apple Pay and Google Pay for one-tap purchases on supported devices, making it possible to top up your balance in under ten seconds.

Support Chat. Our in-app support chat is fully functional on mobile. Whether you have a question about billing, need help with a model, or want to report an issue, the support button is always accessible from the bottom-right corner of the screen. The chat window is draggable and resizable even on mobile, and messages sync across devices so you can start a conversation on your phone and continue it on desktop.

API Key Management. Generate, revoke, and manage API keys from your phone. Each key card shows usage statistics, the last used timestamp, and any spend limits you've set. You can copy the full key to your clipboard with a single tap — no more squinting at long alphanumeric strings and typing them manually.

Usage Analytics. Track your token consumption by model, by time period, and by API key. The charts are interactive and touch-friendly — pinch to zoom, tap to see exact values, swipe between time ranges. You can see at a glance which models are consuming the most tokens and set up spending alerts directly from the mobile interface.

Notifications. Receive real-time push notifications for low balance warnings, successful top-ups, API key usage alerts, and new model announcements. Since the app runs in your browser, notifications work even when the tab is closed (on supported browsers). Never run out of tokens unexpectedly again.

Designed for Mobile First

The mobile web app isn't just the desktop site squeezed into a smaller viewport. We built it mobile-first from the ground up. Every interaction was reconsidered for touch: buttons are larger, forms use native input types (numeric keyboards for amounts, email keyboards for login fields), and navigation uses a bottom-sheet drawer that's thumb-friendly.

We paid special attention to performance. The mobile app loads in under two seconds on 4G connections, with critical CSS inlined and JavaScript lazy-loaded. Images are served in next-gen formats (WebP) at appropriate resolutions for each device. The result is a snappy, native-feeling experience that doesn't drain your battery or eat up your data plan.

Dark mode is fully supported and automatically matches your system preference. The entire interface uses system fonts (San Francisco on iOS, Roboto on Android) for maximum readability at small sizes. Touch targets are at least 44x44 points, exceeding Apple's Human Interface Guidelines and Google's Material Design recommendations.

What's Still in Development

The beta launch covers the core functionality, but we're actively working on several features that didn't make the initial cut:

  • Offline mode — cached dashboard data so you can view your balance and recent transactions even without an internet connection
  • Biometric authentication — Face ID and fingerprint unlock for faster, more secure login
  • Widget support — home screen widgets showing your current token balance at a glance
  • QR code payments — scan to pay from partnered vendors and physical points of sale
  • Voice commands — "Hey GlbTOKEN, how many tokens do I have left?"

How to Get Started

Getting started with the mobile web app couldn't be simpler:

  1. Open Safari, Chrome, Firefox, or any modern browser on your phone or tablet.
  2. Navigate to glbtoken.com and log in with your existing account credentials. If you don't have an account yet, sign up free — it takes less than a minute.
  3. That's it. You'll see the mobile-optimized dashboard automatically. For the best experience, Android users can tap "Add to Home Screen" from the browser menu to install the PWA.

All existing users are automatically enrolled in the beta. There's nothing to install, no separate app to find, and no beta sign-up form to fill out. Just log in from your phone and the mobile interface appears automatically. Your balance, API keys, and history are all right where you left them.

We Want Your Feedback

This is a beta, and that means your feedback is critical. We want to know what works, what doesn't, and what's missing. Every piece of feedback — whether it's a broken layout on a specific device, a feature you'd love to see, or a performance issue — helps us make the mobile app better for everyone.

You can share feedback directly through the in-app support chat (the 💬 button in the bottom-right corner). Alternatively, email us at contact@glbtoken.com with screenshots, device details, and your suggestions. We read every message and prioritize fixes based on user impact.

For beta testers who report significant bugs or contribute valuable feature suggestions, we're offering 500 bonus tokens as a thank-you. Just mention "Mobile Beta" in your support ticket or email, and if your report leads to a fix or your suggestion gets implemented, we'll credit your account.

What's Next After Beta

The beta period will run for approximately 4-6 weeks, during which we'll be iterating rapidly based on user feedback. After that, we'll move to general availability with the following priorities:

  • Stabilizing the core experience based on real-world usage data
  • Shipping the features listed in our development roadmap above
  • Performance optimization for low-end devices and slower networks
  • Accessibility improvements based on WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines
  • Full internationalization with all languages currently supported on desktop

Depending on community interest, we may also explore a lightweight desktop Electron app or a dedicated Telegram mini-app for even faster access. But for now, we're focused on making the mobile web experience the best it can be.

We believe that managing your AI token balance should be as easy as checking your bank balance — accessible anywhere, anytime, on any device. The mobile web app beta is a big step toward that vision, and we're excited to have you along for the ride. Sign up or log in from your phone and let us know what you think.
