Frequently Asked Questions

What is GlbTOKEN?

GlbTOKEN is a global token that gives you access to 100+ AI models through a single balance. Buy once, use across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, and more — no separate subscriptions needed.

How do I buy tokens?

Create an account, go to the Buy Tokens page, select a package or enter a custom amount (minimum $2), and pay with Stripe (card), Paystack (African mobile money/bank transfer), or Crypto (USDT/BTC/ETH).

Do tokens expire?

No! Your GlbTOKEN never expire. Use them whenever you want, across any of our supported AI models.

Which AI models are supported?

100+ models from 14+ providers including OpenAI (GPT-5.5, GPT-4o), Anthropic (Claude 3.5, Sonnet 5), Google (Gemini 3.5, 3.1), Meta (Llama 4), DeepSeek, Mistral, Qwen, Cohere, Perplexity, and more. Check our AI Models page for the full list.

How do I use the API?

Generate an API key from your Dashboard. Use our OpenRouter-compatible endpoint: POST to https://api.glbtoken.io/v1/chat/completions with your key in the Authorization header. Full docs available in the API Keys page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Stripe (international credit/debit cards in USD), Paystack (African payments in NGN, GHS, KES via bank transfer, USSD, and mobile money), and Crypto (USDT TRC-20/ERC-20, BTC, ETH).
